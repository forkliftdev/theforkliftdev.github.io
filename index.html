About

The story

Age is just a data type. I'm 59 years old, I operate a forklift for a living three days a week, and I built a word game platform from scratch using YouTube, Stack Overflow, and a lot of stubbornness.

TheForklift.dev LLC develops and publishes word games for mobile and web. The FlexWord family launched on Reddit via Devvit in 2026 and is expanding to iOS and Android.
